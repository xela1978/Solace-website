Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Solace Brazilian Steakhouse & Bar (“we,” “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website solacebraziliansteakhouse.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit. They help the site remember your preferences, improve performance, and sometimes support analytics or advertising.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies only as needed to make the website work and to understand how it is used. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites for advertising.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary. Required for the site to function (e.g. security, load balancing). These do not require your consent under most privacy laws.
  • Functional. Remember choices you make (e.g. language or region) to improve your experience.
  • Analytics. If we use tools like Google Analytics, they may set cookies to count visits and see how pages are used. We use this only to improve our website.

We do not sell your data. Any analytics we use are configured to respect your privacy as much as possible.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may include content or links from third parties (e.g. Google Maps, social networks, fonts). Those services may set their own cookies. We do not control those cookies; please check their privacy and cookie policies.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies; note that blocking all cookies may affect how our website works (e.g. preferences may not be saved).
